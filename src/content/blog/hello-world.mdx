# Hello World!

Welcome to my blog. This is my first post — a quick look at the tech choices behind this portfolio site.

## Why Next.js?

I wanted a framework that gave me:

- **Server-side rendering** for SEO
- **File-based routing** for simplicity
- **Great developer experience** with TypeScript support out of the box

Next.js checked every box.

## The Design Philosophy

Bold, colorful, and fast. I used **Tailwind CSS** for utility-first styling and **Framer Motion** for scroll-triggered animations that bring the page to life without feeling heavy.
